Privacy Policy & Terms

1. Data We Collect

Essential: No personal data is collected without your consent. We use Firebase Firestore to store anonymous image like counts (not tied to individual users).

2. Cookies & Consent

Your Choice: We only use analytics and functional cookies after you explicitly accept them. You can withdraw consent at any time using the cookie settings button.

Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (session management, security).

Functional Cookies: Enable the "like" feature using localStorage. If rejected, likes are disabled.

Analytics Cookies: Firebase Analytics (Google) - only loaded after consent. Used to understand visitor behavior.

3. Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (though we don't store personal identifiers)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing
  • Request deletion (where technically feasible)

Important: Like data is stored as anonymous aggregates per image. We cannot identify or delete a specific user's likes without a device-specific identifier.

4. Data Retention

Anonymous like counts are stored indefinitely to maintain gallery popularity rankings. Cookie preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage.

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): Analytics data may be transferred to the US under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

CDNs: Google Fonts and Font Awesome are loaded from external CDNs for performance.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests: then-onconformist@outlook.com
